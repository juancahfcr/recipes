Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of bread
 - 1 slice of tomatoe
 - 1 slice of turkey breast
 - 1 slice of cheese (provologne)
 - 1 teaspoon ketchup

Directions:
 1. Put one slice of bread
 2. Add slice turkey breast
 3. Add slice of cheese
 4. Add slice of tomatoe
 5. Add teaspoon ketchup
 6. Add the other slice of bread on top
 7. Enjoy!
